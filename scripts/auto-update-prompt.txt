PinTheft is a Linux kernel local privilege escalation: an RDS (Reliable
Datagram Sockets) zero-copy double-free in net/rds/message.c, turned into
a page-cache overwrite of a SUID-root binary via io_uring fixed buffers.

yet — the tracker uses the placeholder CVE-2026-XXXXX.
